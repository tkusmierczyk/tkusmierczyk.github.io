Tomasz Kuśmierczyk: About me

Hi! My name is Tomasz. I am a computer science researcher. My interests are related to machine learning, statistics and data analytics. I work at the University of Helsinki in Multi-source probabilistic inference group, where my goal is to improve methods for machine learning inference.
